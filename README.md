# Retail-Sales-Analysis
Analyzed Retail Sales using a simulated dataset with 1000 transactions using My SQL Workbench. 
The total revenue generated from the 1000 trannsactions was $456000.
A classification of the different product categories(Beauty, Clothing, Electronics) purchased by different genders (primarily male and female in this dataset) was found along with thier respective demand.
The demand for 'Beauty' as a product category was with their respective gender and age was found.
The total revenue across the product categories was found. Where Electronics gained the highest revenue with $156905.
The average age that purchases the products which can be classified into male and female is: 41.4286 and 41.3569. Even though the difference is minimal but by approximation it can be said that males comparatively have a larger demand however the difference is negligible so no difference in average ages across genders could also be stated.
Top 5 Transaction with from which highest revenue generated was achieved was also found where Transaction ID - 15, 74, 65, 89 and 72 had equal amounts of revenue which they generated as $2000. 
Segregation of maximum revenue generated across different product categories was constant which equals to $2000.
The highest revenue generated by females was $81275 from clothing as a product category.
The highest revenue was generated in the month of May where total sales equals to $53150.
